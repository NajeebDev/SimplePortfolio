Who am I ?

My name is Najeeb Alselwi . I am an aviation professional looking for new challenges, these paragraphs give you a summary about me and my work's success.
I started my job in Yemen Airways in 2006 as Public Relations Officer, I worked in this position for more than five years. I learned many new skills, such as dealing with public, media, and news-press.
In April 2012, my work history and training help me to be promoted to the new job position, Public Relations Supervisor who was responsible for coordinating, developing and implementing all public relations and media activities. Besides, Maintaining and updating information on the company's website. My job responsibilities got a wider scope and I was competent to do my job. In June 2013, I found that I have to change my job to the commercial department since I was well trained.
I worked for more than four years as IATA Affairs Supervisor and staff travel coordinator as well.My new job was interesting, it allows me to coordinate with international organizations like IATA, AACO, ZED/MIBA forum and other Airlines. And I signed many agreements for my company. as I was admin and master user for myidtravel system. I was enjoying working in a multi-cultural environment that needs an excellent communicator and well organize.
Now, I am living in Hamburg Area, Germany, and searching for my next challenge, I am well qualified and competent in public relations and international affairs, I will make success for my new company and add new marks to my work life.
